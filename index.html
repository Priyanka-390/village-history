VILLAGE HISTORY RESEARCH

The Village History Project, developed by the Documentation Center of Cambodia, is an effort to promote And publishing the victims' memories. A workshop on strengthening research work was held to train 200 teachers. About how to compile the oral stories of the victims. All teachers will go to interview the victims who are Living in their own commune village, all this will lead to the establishment of a National Historical Commission On the Prevention of Genocide in Cambodia Continuing to Work on Teacher Training and Development of Instructional Materials For genocide education.

Project Description

Project name "Education from the History of Democratic Kampuchea (1975-1979)" at the 6 regional pedagogical centers, the National Institute of Education and public and private higher education institutions for the basic school year,
